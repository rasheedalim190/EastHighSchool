About Us

Vision

East High School envisions a vibrant learning community where students discover their passions, embrace diversity, and prepare to lead with creativity, confidence, and compassion.

Philosophy

  • Student-Centered Learning: Every student has unique talents worth nurturing.
  • Balance of Academics & Arts: Knowledge and creativity go hand-in-hand.
  • Community Spirit: Collaboration and respect build lifelong bonds.
  • Excellence in Diversity: We celebrate individuality while fostering unity.

Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic education that balances academic excellence, artistic expression, athletic achievement, and personal growth. We empower Wildcats to dream big, work hard, and shine both on stage and in life.

Core Values

Creativity

We foster an environment where imagination thrives, encouraging students to explore new ideas and express themselves through the arts, academics, and athletics.

Teamwork

We believe in the power of collaboration, where students support each other’s growth and work together to achieve common goals, both on and off the stage.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all we do, encouraging students to set high standards, embrace challenges, and continuously grow as learners, artists, athletes, and individuals.

Leadership

We empower students to take initiative, inspire others, and lead with integrity, whether in the classroom, on the field, or in the community.

Respect

We cultivate a culture of respect and empathy, where every voice is valued, and diversity is celebrated as a source of strength.

East High Seal - The Mascot

Our mascot, the Wildcat, symbolizes the unstoppable spirit and tenacity of East High School. Fierce yet focused, it represents our unwavering commitment to excellence, resilience, and community pride. The Wildcat’s bold stance and roar capture the energy, passion, and determination that define every East High student — whether on stage, in the classroom, or on the court.


More than just a symbol, the Wildcat reminds us to “Getcha Head in the Game”— to stay driven, united, and ready to face every challenge with courage and heart.
