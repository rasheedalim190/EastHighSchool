Enrollment Requirements & Procedure

Admission for Freshmen

  • Original copy of Form 138 (Report Card) with grades from Grade 1 to Grade 10.
  • Original copy of Form 137 (School Records) with grades from Grade 1 to Grade 10.
  • Original copy of Birth Certificate (PSA).
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character from the previous school.
  • 2x2 ID photos (4 pieces).

Admission for Transferees

  1. Fill up the application form in the admission portal then upload documentary requirements
  2. Select appointment schedule for validation of requirements
  3. Validation and Evaluation of documentary requirements
  4. Applicant will undergo department evaluation and will not take the entrance examination. Result of the evaluation will determine if the applicant is qualified to proceed
  5. Issuance of Notice of Admission (NOA) for qualified applicants
  6. For accepted applicants, secure a medical referral slip from the Health Service Unit and proceed to medical examination
  7. Claim the medical clearance at the Health Service Unit
  8. Submit the registration requirements to the Office of the Campus Registrar to process your enrollment
